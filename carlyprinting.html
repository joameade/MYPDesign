printing

Introduction

Printing nowadays is different then printing in the ancient times, nowadays the only require for printing is a printer and a sheet of paper, but in the ancient times, people carve words on wood and print it out, which made printing very expensive and that the book must be really good to print out.

History

At the 2th century, the Chinese has discoverd how to print, called movable-type printing, which requires three type of materials, paper, ink and wood to carve the words. To print out one page, first put the wood blocks in the correct order, then use the paint brush to cover over the carved wood words, use a paint brush (with ink on it) to cover the blocks, then place a paper above it.
